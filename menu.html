Fresh Tea

Fresh teas are made without a prevailing standard, are handmade and offer a rich palette of flavor defined by local custom and taste.

Flavored Tea

Whether you enjoy the pure flavor of tea, tea that is beautifully blended with a variety of fruits, herbs, spices – or all of the above.

Milk Tea

Milk tea is simply what it sounds like- tea with milk added. This combination creates a smoothness and slight sweetness to normal bitter teas such as black tea.

Fruit Milk Tea

Add fruit flavor to your milk tea by blending a tea base with milk, fruit and fruit juices.

Fruit Juice

Fruit juice is 100% pure juice made from the flesh of fresh fruit or from whole fruit.

Yakult & Yogurt

Yakult is a delicious probiotic fermented milk drink that contains Yakult's exclusive probiotic LcS (L.casei strain Shirota).

Coffee & Chocolate

Try one of our silky smooth coffees and chocolate drinks, made from freshly pulled shots of espresso with steamed milk and topped with thick foam.
